# Privacy Policy

Good Mind Society respects your privacy. We collect only the information needed to respond to inquiries, process donations, and improve our services. We do not sell or share donor data.

## What we collect
- Contact information you submit via forms
- Donation records provided by our payment processor
- Basic analytics to understand site usage

## How we use information
- Respond to messages and partnership requests
- Provide receipts and donation confirmations
- Improve program communications and reporting

## Your choices
You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by emailing us.
