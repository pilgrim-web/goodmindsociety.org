Linkers 2.0

A calm pathway that links learners, mentors, and local support without pressure.

How we start

We begin with listening sessions and a simple intake to understand goals, time, and comfort level.

Why we do this

Many people want to learn but feel isolated or rushed. Linkers 2.0 builds a steady bridge instead of a quick fix.

What it helps

The pathway supports confidence, basic skills, and the habit of showing up when learning feels hard.

Who benefits

Learners, families, mentors, and local organizations benefit from shared expectations and gentle structure.

Linkers pathway stages

Stage 1: Listen

We meet and identify priorities, strengths, and needs.

Stage 2: Pair

We match learners with mentors and set a small weekly rhythm.

Stage 3: Practice

We support short sessions that build confidence and consistency.

Stage 4: Sustain

We hand off tools so learning continues locally.
